# Syllabus

**MS&E 152: Introduction to Decision Analysis** · Stanford University · Summer 2026

**Instructor:** John Mark Agosta · **Lectures:** Mon/Wed (time and room TBD)

## Description

Decision analysis is the discipline of making high-quality decisions under uncertainty. The course covers framing decisions and achieving clarity of action; structuring problems with decision tables, trees, and influence diagrams; probability as a measure of belief, elicitation, and Bayes' rule; principles of choice and value-based thinking; the value of information; risk preference and biases; value tradeoffs (including small probabilities and micromorts); and real options. We close with decision quality, rationality, and ethics in practice.

## Readings
